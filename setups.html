My primary workstation is a self-built desktop PC of several years, running Windows 10 22H2 and Arch Linux through WSL (Windows Subsystem for Linux) side by side, still in a Cooler Master Haf X nearly a decade after the case's release.
  • Motherboard:

    Asus Prime Z270-A. Good OC Board.

  • CPU:

    Intel i7-7700k (near bleeding edge... for 2017). Delidded.

  • RAM:

    2x16GB G.Skill Ripjaw RGB. Hynix C-Die ICs. Overclocked @ DDR4-4000 1.4v

  • GPU:

    Asus Tuf Gaming Nvidia RTX 4070 TI OC, 12GB VRAM. The newest piece.

  • Storage:

    A 256GB 2.5" Samsung SSD, and 2 7200RPM HDDs at 1TB and 4TB.\ Not ideal, but budgetary constraints.

  • Network:

    Netgear Nighthawk and gigabit fiber, plus an unmanaged switch for my game consoles. Netgear Nighthawk I've flashed with openwrt to tinker and wake-on-lan, and have a dynamic DNS setup to remote access my home devices with ease.

  • Other equipment:

    Laptop with Intel i7 and an Nvidia 1050TI for portable work, a Samsung Tab A7, and a Samsung Galaxy S9+ as my phone.
