If you've come to this website from Google through seeing a local business, from LinkedIn, Indeed.com, or from my Resume, you've come to the right place. This is a central hub from where I can host an assortment of professional works, and show you a little bit more about myself.

    Here you can learn about me, my education, a bit of my life and my interests.

  • [ My Setups ]

    Where you can learn about the equipment I use in my day-to-day. My computers' OS, specs and maintenance automations, my software development environment for coding various projects, and a graph of my home network.

  • [ My Github ]

    My personal code respository and the place I can keep an eye on my favorite FOSS projects and tools. I haven't published all of the work I've done, but there's some here, regardless.

    My college work I am looking at declassifying in the near future, after taking measures for my personal safety and privacy, as my Github is very public, whereas this website is more private. I do have a handful of little things that are available to look at there, however.

    Examples of my work on physical hardware. PC building, manual overclocks, game console repair, controller modding, soldering work, all that.
